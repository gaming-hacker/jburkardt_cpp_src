TETRAHEDRON_PROPERTIES
Compute Properties of a Tetrahedron

TETRAHEDRON_PROPERTIES, a C++ code which computes properties, including the centroid, circumsphere, dihedral angles, edge lengths, face angles, face areas, insphere, quality, solid angles, and volume, of a tetrahedron in 3D.
